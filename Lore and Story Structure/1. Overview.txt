# TBD Space Game - Narrative Design Document (NDD)

## Document Purpose
This document serves as the comprehensive narrative "bible" for the TBD Space Game project, providing detailed specifications for all story elements, world-building, and character development. It ensures narrative consistency and guides the integration of story with gameplay mechanics.
