1.0 Setting Overview
===================
The TBD Space Game takes place in a near-future setting where humanity has established its first interstellar colony missions. The narrative spans multiple generations aboard generation ships and early colony settlements, focusing on the evolution of culture, society, and human identity in isolated space environments.

Key Setting Elements:
- Generation ships traveling to nearby star systems (10-20 light years from Earth)
- Limited communication with Earth that degrades over time
- Resource scarcity and environmental challenges
- Evolving cultural and religious frameworks
- Emergence of space-born societal structures
