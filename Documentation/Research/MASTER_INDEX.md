## Document Usage Guidelines

- All documents follow a consistent numbering system for easy cross-referencing
- Citations follow modified APA format with [DOCUMENT:SECTION] pointers
- Each document begins with a summary of key findings
- Controversial or speculative concepts are clearly marked
- Design implications sections follow technical research sections
- Research confidence levels are indicated where appropriate (1-5 scale)
