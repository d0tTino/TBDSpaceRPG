Overview
========

1.1 Core Concept
----------------
The game is a hard sci-fi simulation combining 3D orbital navigation with 2D crew management. Players captain a spacecraft through realistic solar system environments, plotting trajectories and managing ship systems. Simultaneously, they oversee a small crew in a 2D interior view, assigning tasks and handling interpersonal dynamics. The game is structured in chapters, each representing a mission with specific objectives and challenges.

1.2 Gameplay Goals
------------------
Players must:
- Plot and execute spacecraft trajectories using realistic orbital mechanics.
- Manage crew members by assigning roles, tasks, and responding to their needs and relationships.
- Make critical decisions during events like solar flares or system failures, with outcomes affected by crew status and traits.
- Complete mission objectives, such as landing on Mars or mining asteroids, while balancing resources and crew well-being.
- Adapt to the consequences of their actions, as outcomes carry over to subsequent chapters.
